> Huh.  The def'n doesn't say that.  *sigh*

Probably the default is not official, but was sort of decided later.

The idea is to cover all four Aristotelian quantifiers with single
cmavo. The four are All, Some, No and Not All. The first three had
single words in Lojban but the last one didn't, so using me'i for it
was convenient:
All = ro
Some = su'o (pa)
No = no
Not All = me'i (ro)
